NAAMAN

Good mental health is the most important part of coping with stress, interacting with others, and making choices.

Why Choose Us

Our main goal is to help improve people’s mental health in these difficult times by providing a social media platform where people can share their stories and get online consulting from mental health professionals.

Our Properties

Naaman will provide an anonymous platform so that people are able to freely express their doubts and concerns without the fear of being judged by relatives or friends when using the platform.

Our Vision

This web based remote mental health management platform is an online community designed to manage mental health consultations of users. The application provides the features to share thougts, online consultation booking and access to professional help.

Our Vision

Naaman was created in order to provide a social media platform to help people who are experiencing mental health issues.
